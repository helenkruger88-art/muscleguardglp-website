The differentiator

How muscle preservation works on a GLP-1

The single concern that surfaces in every Reddit thread, every clinical paper, and every analysis of online discourse — and the one no other GLP-1 app puts in their headline.

GLP-1 medications suppress appetite so effectively that many users enter a severe calorie and protein deficit. Muscle is built from protein, so a sustained deficit can lead to loss of lean mass — what users call “muscle drain” or “Ozempic face”.

The prevention plan is well-established: aim for 1.2 to 1.6 g of protein per kg of body weight per day, perform resistance training 2 to 4 times per week, avoid extreme calorie restriction, and track strength and body composition — not just weight.

Muscle Guard is built around that plan. Every feature, every milestone, every metric is in service of one thing: fat loss without muscle loss.
