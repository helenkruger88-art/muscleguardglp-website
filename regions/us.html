Frequently asked — United States

Does Muscle Guard work with my Apple Watch / Whoop / Oura?

Apple HealthKit and Google Health Connect are on the roadmap for our next major release. Connection to wearables will be opt-in and transparent.

Is the subscription HSA / FSA eligible?

Not currently. Some users have successfully reimbursed via the app store receipt — confirm with your plan administrator.

Do you integrate with telehealth providers like Ro, Hims, or Sequence?

Not directly yet. The Doctor PDF works with any prescriber, including telehealth — patients have used it with Ro, Hims, Sequence and traditional clinic settings.

How is Muscle Guard different from Weight Watchers Med+?

Weight Watchers Med+ is excellent at the eating and behavioural side. Muscle Guard is the only one built around muscle preservation as the headline — protein adherence, resistance training, body composition, and the Doctor PDF. They're complementary; many users run both.

CCPA — California Consumer Privacy Act?

Yes, we're CCPA-compliant. California residents have full rights to access, deletion, correction, and opt-out of data sale (which is moot — we don't sell data).
