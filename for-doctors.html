What Muscle Guard is — and is not

Muscle Guard is a patient self-tracking companion and coach. It is not a medical device, does not provide diagnostic information, does not recommend dose changes, and does not substitute for clinical judgement. It is a structured record of what the patient is doing between consults — formatted for your eye.

Privacy and regulatory posture

  • POPIA-compliant (South Africa). GDPR-compliant (EU). CCPA-compliant (California).
  • No selling of patient data. No third-party advertising trackers.
  • Patient can delete account and all associated data in one tap.
  • Clinical Report is generated on-device and shared at the patient's discretion only.
