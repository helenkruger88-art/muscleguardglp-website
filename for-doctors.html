What Muscle Guard is — and is not

Muscle Guard is a patient self-tracking companion. It is not a medical device, does not provide diagnostic information, does not recommend dose changes, and does not substitute for clinical judgement. It is a structured record of what the patient is doing between consults — formatted for your eye.
