Common questions

How do I delete my account?

In the app: Profile → Delete Account → confirm twice. All data is permanently removed within 7 days. Or visit our account deletion page.

Is Muscle Guard a medical device?

No. Muscle Guard is a self-tracking companion app. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult your prescriber.

How do I cancel Pro?

Through your App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) subscription settings. Access continues until the end of your current billing period.
