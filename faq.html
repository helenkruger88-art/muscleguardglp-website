Is Muscle Guard a medical device?

No. Muscle Guard is a self-tracking companion app for people on GLP-1 medications. It is not a medical device, does not provide diagnostic information, and does not substitute for medical advice. Always consult your prescriber for clinical decisions.

Which GLP-1 medications does Muscle Guard support?

Ozempic and Wegovy (semaglutide), Mounjaro and Zepbound (tirzepatide), Rybelsus (oral semaglutide), Saxenda (liraglutide), plus compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide preparations. Each drug has its standard titration schedule built in.

Why does muscle preservation matter on a GLP-1?

GLP-1 medications suppress appetite so effectively that many users enter a severe calorie and protein deficit. Without intervention, 25 to 40 percent of the weight you lose can come from lean mass — what users call "muscle drain". Resistance training plus 1.2 to 1.6 g/kg/day of protein dramatically reduces that risk.

How much does Muscle Guard cost?

Free forever for the basics. Pro is R99/month or R899/year (about USD 5.99 / EUR 5.99 / GBP 4.99 in equivalent regions) with a 7-day free trial — no card needed to start.

How do I cancel Pro?

Through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) subscription settings. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

In-app: Profile → Delete Account → confirm twice. All data is permanently removed within 7 days. If you've already lost access, email support@muscleguardglp.com from your account email and we'll delete it for you.

Does Muscle Guard work with Apple Watch, Whoop or Oura?

Apple HealthKit and Google Health Connect integration is on the roadmap for the next major release. It will read steps, sleep, heart rate and workout data — all opt-in, all transparent.

What about my data privacy?

We never sell your data. No third-party advertising trackers. POPIA, GDPR and CCPA compliant. Your data lives on your device and our servers (encrypted), and you can delete all of it in one tap. See our Privacy Policy.

Where is the AI plate scan data sent?

The image is sent to Google Gemini for macro and ingredient detection. The image is not stored after processing. Open Food Facts is queried for packaged-food data. All of this is disclosed in our Privacy Policy.

Is Muscle Guard available in my country?

The app is available worldwide. ZAR is the primary displayed currency by default; switch via the regional toggle on the home page to USD, EUR or GBP. Country-specific medical aid / insurance information varies — see /regions for details.

Can I use Muscle Guard with compounded semaglutide or tirzepatide?

Yes. Compounded preparations are first-class citizens in Muscle Guard — particularly relevant in South Africa where branded products are often unaffordable. You can log compounded vials with custom dose schedules.

My GLP-1 has stopped working — what now?

Plateaus and tachyphylaxis are common. Talk to your prescriber. In the meantime, your Doctor PDF will show the full picture of what changed and when, which makes the consult more productive. Track plateaus, dose changes, and side effects in Muscle Guard so you and your prescriber can spot the pattern together.

How is Muscle Guard different from Shotsy, MeAgain or Weight Watchers Med+?

Muscle Guard is the only one built around muscle preservation as the headline. We track injections like the others, but we also track protein adherence, resistance training, body composition, and produce the Doctor PDF that turns your data into something a prescriber will actually use.
