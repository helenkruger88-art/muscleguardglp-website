Delete your Muscle Guard account

How to permanently delete your Muscle Guard account and all associated data — in-app in 30 seconds, or via email if you've already lost access.

⚕ Brand Expert (Pty) Ltd · Last updated 19 May 2026

If you still have access to the app

  1. Open Muscle Guard on your phone.
  2. Go to Profile (the silhouette icon, bottom right).
  3. Tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm twice — once on the warning screen, once on the final confirmation.

All your personal data is permanently deleted from our systems within 7 days. Aggregated, anonymised analytics may be retained for app improvement.

If you have already lost access

Email support@muscleguardglp.com from the email address registered to your account. We will verify your identity and delete the account within 7 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account record and login credentials
  • All medication logs, weight entries, body measurements, photos, mood logs, side effect records, training sessions, and meal data
  • All Doctor PDFs you have generated
  • All milestones and achievement records
  • Your subscription record (if any). Cancel any active subscription through App Store or Google Play before deleting to ensure no further charges.

What is retained

  • Anonymised, aggregated metrics used to improve the app (cannot be linked back to you)
  • Records required by South African tax or financial regulation, retained for the statutory minimum period

Need help?

If you have any questions about the deletion process or want confirmation of completed deletion, email support@muscleguardglp.com.
